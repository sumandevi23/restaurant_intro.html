About Restaurent

Benares restaurant in New York has an emotions overtone about its name after the holy city of Baranasi, the menu of Benares includes both veg and non-veg dishesfrom Kashmir to Kanyakumari. The inerior decor of Teribeca restaurant is themed with Banarasi saris of golden and silver hues. Tibet's spicy lamb soup, Kashmir's lamb rohan josh and Varanasi's lauki ka kofta and south Indian's chicken curry with coconut milk are some the must have dishes at the best Indian restaurant NYC.
